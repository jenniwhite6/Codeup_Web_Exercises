National Parks are fab

How old is the National Park system?
President Woodrow Wilson signed an act creating the National Park Service in 1916. (Yellowstone was created before the act was signed, in 1872, thanks to Ulysses S. Grant. Great guy.)
Which National Park is the largest?
Wrangell-St. Elias National Park and Preserve, AK, at 13.2 million acres
Which National Park is the smallest?
Thaddeus Kosciuszko National Memorial, PA, at 0.02 acres
